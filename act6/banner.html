Drama is the fourth extended play by South Korean girl group Aespa. It was released by SM Entertainment on November 10, 2023, and contains six tracks including the lead single of the same name. According to SM Entertainment, Drama will showcase aespa evolving personalities with "fresh music" while "also building upon on a previous narrative thread as the group is now breaking out from the trauma caused by a series of events with 'Synk Out', and 'Hallucination Quest' in the SMCU's storyline for Aespa.
